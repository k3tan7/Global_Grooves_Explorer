About Global Grooves

Discover radio stations from around the world

What is Global Grooves?

Global Grooves is your gateway to live radio stations from across the world. Whether you're interested in discovering local music, news, or talk shows from different cultures, our platform makes it easy to explore and listen to broadcast stations from any part of the globe.

Features

  • ✓ Browse Stations – Access a constantly updated database of radio stations from different countries
  • ✓ Search & Filter – Easily find stations by name, country, or language
  • ✓ Save Favorites – Build a personalized collection of your favorite stations
  • ✓ Dark Mode – Comfortable listening experience day and night

Data Source

Our station data is powered by Radio Browser, a community-driven database of radio stations worldwide. This ensures we have access to the most current and diverse selection of broadcasters.

Privacy

Your favorite stations are saved locally on your device. We don't collect personal data, track your listening habits, or use external analytics. Your privacy is our priority.
